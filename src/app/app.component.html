IndabaX Gabon 2025 is the first edition of the Deep Learning IndabaX in Gabon, bringing together researchers, developers, and AI enthusiasts. The event fosters knowledge sharing, networking, and collaboration to drive AI innovation in Gabon and across Africa.

Topics to Discuss

  • Core AI & Machine Learning
  • AI in Healthcare
  • AI & Climate Change
  • AI in Business & Finance
  • AI for Social Good & Ethics
  • AI Startups & Industry Applications
  • Hands-on Workshops & Hackathons
